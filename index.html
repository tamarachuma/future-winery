We try to keep our footprint on the land as light as possible. Using techniques such as removing leaves in the fruit zone, in the correct amount, at the correct time, can help in reducing fungal diseases which are the bane of all grape growers. Grape growing, as with all farming, is successful when you know what you can control and what you cannot. Picking the best site, keeping up with the technology available, and using a gentle hand helps us get the best Mother Nature has to offer.
